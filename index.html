How It Works

Just Gym email used to run through IONOS — a basic email hosting service. We've migrated to Google Workspace, which ties your email directly into Gmail, Drive, Calendar, and automation tools.

Active Setup

📬 info@just-gym.com

This is the one and only email account. It lives in Google Workspace. All email, Drive files, Calendar, and automations run through this account.

The short version: IONOS was just a mailbox. Google Workspace is a full operating system for the business.

The two info@ Google accounts

There are two separate Google accounts that both use info@just-gym.com. They look similar but are completely different things. Always use the Workspace one for anything business-related.

Personal Google Account
Free consumer account — created by signing up at google.com with the info@ address
Has its own Google Drive (personal files, not business)
Not managed by any admin — fully personal
No business email — does not handle gym inbox
Exists separately from the business setup
⚠️ Do not use this for gym work
Workspace Account
Paid business account — managed through Google Workspace
This is the actual Just Gym email inbox (Gmail)
Google Drive here holds all business files
Connected to Claude, Zapier, and automations
Has admin controls and business features
✓ Always use this one for gym work
How to tell which one you're in
  • ✓
    Workspace account — When you go to Gmail you see the Just Gym inbox. At the top-right of any Google page, click your avatar and it will show a small "Managed by" or "Google Workspace" label under the email.
  • !
    Personal account — Gmail will be empty or show personal emails. No "Managed by" label. Drive will show personal files only. If this is what you see, switch accounts immediately.

When Chrome shows two info@just-gym.com options in the account switcher, pick the one labelled Workspace or showing the Just Gym admin domain.

Chrome Sign-In — Always Check

🔑 Rule: Chrome must be signed into info@just-gym.com

Every time you open Chrome to do anything gym-related, check the profile icon in the top-right corner first. It must show info@just-gym.com.

  • Open Chrome — look at the top-right corner for the profile circle.
  • Click the circle — it shows the name or initial of the signed-in account.
  • Confirm it says info@just-gym.com. If not, click "Switch account" and select it.
  • Then proceed with whatever you need to do — Gmail, Drive, Calendar, forms, bookings.

Why this matters

If you're signed in as a personal Google account or an old account, any Google Forms you submit, Drive files you upload, or calendar events you create will go to the wrong place — or be inaccessible to the business.

Quick Check — Before Any Task
  • 1
    Chrome profile = info@just-gym.com Check the circle in the top-right before starting.
  • 2
    Gmail open? You should see the Just Gym inbox at mail.google.com — not a personal inbox.
  • 3
    Drive open? drive.google.com should show Just Gym files, not personal files.
  • 4
    Booking / form submissions? Always done while signed in as info@ so responses land in the right Sheet.

Claude Desktop — Connected to Google

Claude on desktop is connected directly to your Google account — Gmail, Drive, and Calendar. This means you can ask Claude to do real tasks using your actual business data, not just generic answers.

🤖 What you can ask Claude

Because Claude has access to your Google Drive and Gmail, you can give it real context — not just "write me an email" but "write me a reply based on this."

  • "Draft a reply to the membership enquiry in my inbox, keep it friendly and use the pricing from the membership doc in Drive."
  • "Look at the class timetable in Drive and write an email telling members about this week's schedule."
  • "Check my Gmail for any unanswered enquiries from the last 3 days and summarise them."
  • "Using the waiver template in Drive, fill it in for a new member and save it."
  • "Write a follow-up email for anyone who submitted the trial class form this week."

How the connection works

Claude Desktop connects to Google via your info@just-gym.com account. It can read files from Google Drive, read and draft emails in Gmail, and pull context from Docs and Sheets — so its answers are specific to Just Gym, not generic.

📂
Drive Access
Claude can read any file in your Drive — templates, timetables, pricing docs — and use them as context.
📬
Gmail Access
Claude can read your inbox and draft replies, saving you time on routine email responses.
✍️
Draft & Save
Ask Claude to draft a doc or email — it can save it back to Drive or put it in Gmail drafts ready to send.
🔍
Find Anything
Ask Claude to find a specific email, file, or piece of info across your Google account instantly.
Important

Claude uses whichever Google account Chrome is signed into

This is another reason to always keep Chrome signed into info@just-gym.com. If you're on the wrong account, Claude will be reading the wrong Drive and the wrong inbox.
